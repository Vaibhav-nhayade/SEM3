Generate quizzes quickly from a prompt, file, or document. This demo is front-end only and shows how the UI will look.

How It Works

1. Enter a prompt

Provide a meaningful, specific prompt or topic. Clear prompts produce better question sets.

2. Specify quiz details

Select question types (MCQ, Fill-in-the-blank, True/False) and number of questions required.

3. Review and share

Check generated questions, edit if needed, and distribute to students or peers.

Benefits of AI Quiz Generator

Save Time

Generate quizzes instantly using AI and cut down hours of manual work.

Multiple Question Types

Support for MCQs, T/F, short answers and more to fit course needs.

Versatile Input

Use a prompt, upload a file, or provide a document to generate quizzes.

Easy Customization

Edit and refine generated questions to suit your syllabus and difficulty level.

AI Quiz Generator FAQ

1. How accurate are the AI-generated questions?

The quality depends on the input. Clear, specific prompts or structured documents yield the best results. Always review before sharing.

2. Can I choose question types and difficulty?

Yes — specify types like MCQ, Fill-in-the-blank, True/False and also add instructions about difficulty in your prompt.

3. Is student data or answers stored?

This demo is front-end only (no backend). In a real product, follow best practices and relevant privacy rules to protect student data.
